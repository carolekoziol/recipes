### Ingredients

**Crust**

- 1 1/2 cups graham crackers (dairy-free)
- 1/4 cup vegan butter, or coconut oil, melted 

**Filling**

- 1 1/2 cups raw cashew, soaked
- 1 cup silken tofu
- 1/2 cup [heavy cream](../sauces/vegan_heavy_cream.md) or full-fat coconut milk
